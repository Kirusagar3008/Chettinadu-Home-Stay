About Us

Discover the timeless charm of Chettinadu at our homestay in Chidambaram. We blend traditional South Indian hospitality with modern comforts, offering an authentic experience inspired by the region's opulent heritage and renowned cuisine.

Our restored property, Balambigai Illam, features spacious, elegantly decorated rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, free WiFi, dining areas, TVs, and wardrobes. Guests can relax on the terrace or lounge area, enjoying serene surroundings near the iconic Nataraja Temple—perfect for cultural explorers, food enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

The property includes essential facilities like a shared kitchen, daily housekeeping, free on-site parking, a 24-hour front desk, concierge, and room service. A vegetarian breakfast is served daily, with free toiletries, a refrigerator, and a dining table provided for added convenience.

Located just 67 km from Puducherry Airport, CHETTINADU HOMESTAY offers easy access to local attractions. Indulge in fresh, home-cooked Chettinadu specialties that capture the essence of this vibrant culinary tradition, ensuring a truly memorable stay.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time? +

Check-in time is from 12:00 PM onwards. Check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate? +

Yes, a traditional vegetarian breakfast is included in all room rates. We can also accommodate special dietary requirements with prior notice.

Do you provide airport pickup? +

We can arrange airport pickup from Pondicherry Airport (67 km) for an additional charge. Please contact us in advance to arrange transportation.

Are pets allowed? +

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets at our homestay to maintain a comfortable environment for all guests.

What languages do you speak? +

Our staff speaks English and Tamil. We can also arrange for local guides who speak other languages if needed.

Is there parking available? +

Yes, free on-site parking is available for all guests. No reservation is required.

Can I cancel or modify my booking? +

You can cancel or modify your booking up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund. Please contact us directly for cancellations.

Do you offer cooking classes? +

Yes, we offer traditional Chettinad cooking classes as part of our cultural packages. These can be arranged for individuals or groups.
