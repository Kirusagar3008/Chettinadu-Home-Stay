About Us

Discover the timeless charm of Chettinadu at our homestay in Chidambaram. We blend traditional South Indian hospitality with modern comforts, offering an authentic experience inspired by the region's opulent heritage and renowned cuisine.

Our restored property, Balambigai Illam, features spacious, elegantly decorated rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, free WiFi, dining areas, TVs, and wardrobes. Guests can relax on the terrace or lounge area, enjoying serene surroundings near the iconic Nataraja Templeâ€”perfect for cultural explorers, food enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

The property includes essential facilities like a shared kitchen, daily housekeeping, free on-site parking, a 24-hour front desk, concierge, and room service. A vegetarian breakfast is served daily, with free toiletries, a refrigerator, and a dining table provided for added convenience.

Located just 67 km from Puducherry Airport, CHETTINADU HOMESTAY offers easy access to local attractions. Indulge in fresh, home-cooked Chettinadu specialties that capture the essence of this vibrant culinary tradition, ensuring a truly memorable stay.

Hosted by AL Ganesan

Balambigai Illam is thoughtfully designed to provide guests with a serene stay, ensuring comfort, safety, and a homely atmosphere. Guests can enjoy authentic Chettinadu cuisine and experience the warmth of a traditional CHETTINADU HOMESTAY that truly feels like home.

CHETTINADU HOMESTAY was founded by Mr. AL Ganesan (ALG) with a vision to share the essence of traditional living with visitors to Chidambaram. Inspired by this passion, the homestay has been carefully structured in association with his IT firm to provide guests with a well-organized and secure stay. With Tourism Board registration, GST compliance, and all essential regulations in place, guests are assured of a safe, comfortable, and regulation-compliant accommodation experience.
